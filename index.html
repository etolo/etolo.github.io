Data science / econometrics / statistics:

    Money market:

    The first article shows that interbank loans can contain private information not present in CDS prices, and that such private information is most likely to be relevant for weaker banks at times of economic turbulence. The second article investigates how the differential interbank loan pricing of large and small banks may reflect too-big-to-fail expectations.

  • Tölö, E., Jokivuolle, E., Virén, M., 2017, Do banks' overnight borrowing rates lead their CDS Price? Journal of Financial Intermediation, Vol. 31, pp. 93–106. Available at: https://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.jfi.2017.05.006

  • Tölö, E., Jokivuolle, E., Virén, M., Has Banks’ Monitoring of other Banks Strengthened Post-crisis? Evidence from the European Overnight Market (submitted). Working paper available at: http://urn.fi/URN:NBN:fi:bof-201909251502

    • Crises:

    Following 5 articles all provide evidenve that financial crises are to signicant extent predictable using economic indicators. The articles contribute as to what are the indicators and models give most accurate predictions. Gated recurrent neural networks seem to give most accurate predictions.

  • Tölö, E., 2020, Predicting financial crises with recurrent neural networks, Journal of Financial Stability, Volume 49, August 2020, 100746. Available at: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jfs.2020.100746

  • Tölö, E., Laakkonen, H., Kalatie, S., 2018, Indicators used in setting the countercyclical capital buffer, International Journal of Central Banking, Vol. 14, No. 2, pp. 51–111. Available at: https://www.ijcb.org/journal/ijcb18q1a2.htm

  • Virtanen, T., Tölö, E., Virén, M., Taipalus, K., 2018, Can bubble theory foresee financial crises? Journal of Financial Stability, Vol. 36, pp. 66–81. Available at: https://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.jfs.2018.02.008

  • Kauko, K., Tölö, E., 2020, On the Long-Run Calibration of the Credit-to-GDP Gap as a Banking Crisis Predictor, Finnish Economic Papers (forthcoming). Working paper available at: http://urn.fi/URN:NBN:fi:bof-201902251071

  • Kauko, K., Tölö, E., 2020, Crisis prediction with differenced relative credit, Applied Economics Quarterly (forthcoming). Working paper available at: http://urn.fi/URN:NBN:fi:bof-201906061225
